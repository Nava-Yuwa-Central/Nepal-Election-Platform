# Who's My Neta - User Guide

## Website Overview

**Purpose:** Who's My Neta is a civic engagement platform that empowers Nepali citizens to rate, discuss, and evaluate emerging Gen Z leaders ahead of Nepal's March 2026 election. Users can upvote or downvote leaders, view their manifestos, and engage in public discussions about their agendas.

**Access:** Public - no login required to browse and vote

**Language Support:** English and Nepali (toggle in top navigation)

---

## Powered by Manus

This platform is built with cutting-edge technology: **React 19 + TypeScript** frontend with Tailwind CSS for responsive design, **Node.js/Express + tRPC** backend for type-safe APIs, **MySQL database** for structured data persistence, and **Manus OAuth** for secure authentication. The application features auto-scaling infrastructure with global CDN deployment for optimal performance and reliability.

## Using Your Website

### Discovering Leaders

Visit the **"Leaders"** page to browse all featured Gen Z leaders. Each leader card displays their name, affiliation, biography, and current approval rating. Click any leader card to view their full profile.

### Rating Leaders

On a leader's profile page, you'll see their **approval rating meter** showing the percentage of positive votes. Click the **"Upvote"** button to express approval or **"Downvote"** to express disapproval. You can change your vote at any time, and voting is anonymous—no login required.

### Reading Manifestos and Agendas

Each leader's profile includes their **manifesto** (policy statement) and **agendas** (specific issues they champion). Scroll down to read their full vision and priorities for Nepal's future.

### Joining Discussions

At the bottom of each leader's profile, you'll find the **"Discussions"** section. Enter your name (optional), type your comment, and click **"Post"** to share your thoughts. Read what others are saying and engage in civic dialogue.

### Checking the Leaderboard

Visit the **"Leaderboard"** page to see the top-rated leaders ranked by net approval score. The leaderboard updates in real-time as votes are cast.

### Searching Leaders

Use the search bar on the **"Leaders"** page to find leaders by name, region, or affiliation. Filter by region using the region buttons to narrow results.

## Managing Your Website

### Changing Language

Click the language toggle in the top-right corner (EN / नेपाली) to switch between English and Nepali. Your preference is saved automatically.

### Viewing Your Vote

Your votes are stored locally on your device. If you return to a leader's profile, your previous vote will be highlighted.

### Accessing the Dashboard

Project administrators can access the **Management UI** (via the dashboard panel) to view analytics, manage content visibility, and monitor platform health.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to log in to vote?**  
A: No. Voting is anonymous and open to all visitors. You can vote without creating an account.

**Q: Can I change my vote?**  
A: Yes. Click the same vote button again to change or remove your vote.

**Q: Are comments moderated?**  
A: Comments are public and should be respectful. Abusive content may be removed.

**Q: Why do some leaders show "Verified"?**  
A: Verified leaders have been reviewed and approved by the Who's My Neta team. Unverified leaders are pending review.

**Q: How often is the leaderboard updated?**  
A: The leaderboard updates in real-time as votes are cast.
